General Information

RijnMUN is a Model United Nations conference hosted by students of Het Rijnlands Lyceum Oegstgeest. It is a small conference aimed for beginner delegates to familiarize themselves with the MUN rules of procedure and get involved with the MUN community. RijnMUN follows the THIMUN rules of procedures, which can be found here.

How can I be part of (Rijn)MUN?

  • Register for RijnMUN! We are a beginner-friendly conference and in the two-day event you will receive workshops to help you find your way in the MUN community! Make sure you follow all the steps in the registration page.
  • Check out the issues you will be discussing in your committee on the Committees and Issues page. Make sure to read the research reports your chairs have written for you.
  • Find out more about the country which you are representing: we recommend using the CIA World Factbook or the list of UN member states as a starting point!
  • Understand the stance of your country on the issues that you will be discussing. Is your country in favor or against a certain solution? Are there already alliances with certain countries in order to solve the issue?
  • Prepare clauses, resolutions and or policy statements: check out the guides provided by the THIMUN foundation to help you!

    Guidelines

  • All must abide by the THIMUN guidelines on top of the following RijnMUN specific guidelines below:
  • Delegates must respect all other staff and delegates associated with the conference;
  • Visitors will only be allowed into the conference with a badge, which will be distributed on the first day upon arrival;
  • All people associated with RijnMUN must treat the building and corresponding properties with respect and care. Anyone caught in violation of this rule will be held accountable by the RijnMUN Board of Directors, who will decide upon appropriate consequences;
  • Wear appropriate attire. Males shall wear a suit, tie, and formal footwear, while female delegates are expected to wear formal clothing, for e.g. a blouse and appropriate length skirt.

    About RLO

    Rijnlands Lyceum Oegstgeest is part of the Het Rijnlands Lyceum Foundation first established in 1936. RLO was first founded in 1956 as a Dutch secondary school. It soon founded its TTO (bilingual) education and IB programs in the 1990s.

    Having HAVO, VWOo, gymnasium, TTO and IB programs all under the same roof, Het Rijnlands Lyceum Oegstgeest is a unique and diverse place for students to be in. Having over 1400 students, amongst them 350 of which are international students from over 40 countries; and 150 staff members, RLO is home to many wonderful opportunities.
