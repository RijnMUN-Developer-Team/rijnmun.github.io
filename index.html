10th to 12th October 2025

What is MUN? See the issues Agenda

Dear MUN directors, aspiring delegates, students officers, and all others,

The Board of Directors of RijnMUN 2025 is overjoyed to announce the continuation of Het Rijnlands Lyceum Oegstgeest Model United Nations (RijnMUN), following our inaugral conference in October of 2024, which successfully hosted 170 delegates in 5 different committees. Welcome to our website.

RijnMUN 2025 is a conference organized with a beginner-friendly approach, aiming to promote the fascinating world of MUN to a new generation of delegates and student officers. Throughout our three-day conference we will be offering delegate and student officer workshops for both new and experienced delegates.

Finally, we encourage you to look through our website to learn more about this years' RijnMUN. Please do not hesitate to contact us for any questions you may have.

We hope to see you all there for RijnMUN 2025!

Sincerely,
Alan Gil Forza
Secretary General RijnMUN 2025
