Dear MUN directors, advisors, aspiring delegates and student officers,

On behalf of our Secretaries-General, we are overjoyed to announce the first ever Rijnlands Lyceum Oegstgeest Model United Nations (RijnMUN) hosted at our school in 2024. And so hereby we would like to welcome you to our website.

Over the past months our secretariat has worked effortlessly to build up our conference from point zero, and we are excited to finally be able to share our efforts with you. As many of our neighboring conferences may know, our school has always had an established MUN community. Throughout the past decades our club has attended conferences all over and even outside of the Netherlands. Our MUN directors and students have therefore come into contact and learnt from many conferences held previously to our own. We are absolutely certain that therefore our school, its staff and most importantly its students have both the experience and the enthusiasm that it will take in order to make this conference a success.

Our conference will be hosted in Oegstgeest, a small town in the heart of the Netherlands. We are merely a 6 minute bus ride away from Leiden Centraal which in the center of the beautiful old city making our conference easily accessible and greatly located.

Throughout the duration of our two-day conference we aim to give delegates a great first time experience in which they will be able to learn all about the rules of procedure and what makes a good delegate. We will ensure this is possible through having workshops led by our experienced Board of Directors and by employing diligent student officers who can guide these delegates through their first MUN conference.

For any further information we encourage you to look through our website or contact us for questions you may have. As of now we are eager to receive all your applications. We hope to see you all there in RijnMUN 2024!

Sincerely,
Emily Hanlon and Julie Lodereau
Secretaries-General of RijnMUN 2024
