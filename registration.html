School Registrations

Schools may reserve delegate spots based on the number of beginner delegates expected to join MUN in the new school year. Schools will only need to confirm details of delegates, which may be two more or two less than reserved, in September.

To sign up as a school, please fill in this form.

Please feel free to contact us for any questions or amendments regarding your applications.
